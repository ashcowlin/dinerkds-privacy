Privacy Policy Last updated: 20/02/2026 This application (“the App”) is designed for use in restaurant and kitchen environments to display and manage food orders. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting any information processed through the App. 1. Information We Collect The App does not collect or store any personal information about end users. The App processes the following operational data: - Order details (items, modifiers, timestamps) - Menu data - Internal identifiers used for order routing This data is not linked to any individual person and contains no personal identifiers such as names, email addresses, phone numbers, or location data. 2. How Data Is Stored The App uses Google Firebase to store and sync order and menu data across devices. Firebase may automatically collect: - Device identifiers - Crash logs - Basic analytics (e.g., app opens, performance metrics) This information is handled according to Google’s privacy policies and is not used by the developer for tracking or advertising. 3. How Data Is Used Data is used only for: - Displaying orders to kitchen staff - Updating order status - Synchronizing menu items and order information between devices No data is sold, shared, or used for marketing. 4. Third‑Party Services The App uses the following third‑party services: - Google Firebase (Realtime Database / Firestore / Authentication / Crashlytics) You can review Google’s privacy policy here: https://policies.google.com/privacy 5. Data Security We rely on Firebase’s built‑in security rules to protect data. Only authorized devices connected to your restaurant’s Firebase project can access order data. 6. Children’s Privacy The App is not intended for personal use by children and does not collect any personal information. 7. Changes to This Policy We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted in the same location where this policy is published. 8.
